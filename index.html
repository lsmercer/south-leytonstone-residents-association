About Us

The South Leytonstone Residents Association is a community organization dedicated to representing the interests of residents in South Leytonstone, including both Cann Hall and Cathall, in Waltham Forest, London.

Constitution

Our constitution outlines the rules and objectives of the association to ensure transparency and effective governance. Below is our model constitution:

1. Name

The name of the Association shall be South Leytonstone Residents Association.

2. Objects

The objects of the association shall be to promote and protect the interests of the local residents living in South Leytonstone.

3. Membership

Membership is open to any interested tenant or leaseholder living in the area covered by the association over the age of 16. All members shall have one vote.

4. Subscriptions

No subscription shall be charged for membership. Membership is based solely on where you live.

5. Equal Opportunities

The association will not discriminate on the grounds of nationality, race, gender, disability, sexual orientation, religion or belief.

6. The Committee

The Committee will be elected annually at the AGM and will be responsible for the management of the Association.

7. Annual General Meeting

There shall be an Annual General Meeting held every year at which the annual report and statement of accounts will be presented, and the committee elected.

8. General Meetings

General meetings will be held regularly to update members and discuss important issues.

9. Finance

All monies raised by or on behalf of the Association shall be applied to further the objects and aims of the Association and for no other purpose.

10. Dissolution

The Association may only be dissolved at a Special General Meeting called for that purpose.
